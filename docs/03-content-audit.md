# 03 - Content Audit And Formula System

## Positioning Decision

LyliShop should not compete as "another yarn accessory shop". The stronger position is:

> LyliShop makes small handmade gifts that carry personal color, memory, and care.

This is different from the reference site while learning its strongest strategic idea: handmade products are sold through emotion, not just specifications.

## Reference Content Patterns

| Pattern | Reference use | What to learn | What to avoid |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Brand phrase | Repeats emotional gift tagline. | A short emotional promise helps recall. | Do not repeat vague slogans without product proof. |
| Category grid | Shows product types early. | Give users fast paths by need/category. | Do not create too many categories for small inventory. |
| Best sellers | Highlights popular products. | Make choice easier. | Do not claim best seller without real data. Use "Mẫu nổi bật". |
| Testimonials | Builds social proof. | Trust layer matters. | No fake reviews or unverified names. |
| Blog/news | Educates and supports SEO. | Build topic cluster around gift/handmade/care. | Avoid generic trend posts unrelated to buying. |
| About mission | Explains brand values. | Brand story improves EEAT. | Keep it real; no overclaiming. |
| Policy/footer | Clarifies operations. | Buyers need delivery, privacy, return expectations. | Do not add fake address/email. |

## LyliShop Content Pillars

1. Gift emotion  
   Purpose: sell meaning and memory.

2. Handmade process  
   Purpose: prove effort and uniqueness.

3. Color personalization  
   Purpose: make a small item feel made-for-you.

4. Product guidance  
   Purpose: reduce choice overload.

5. Trust and order clarity  
   Purpose: reduce anxiety before messaging.

6. Care and after-sales  
   Purpose: increase perceived quality and retention.

7. Seasonal gifting  
   Purpose: create timely reasons to buy.

## Content Formula By Funnel Stage

### Awareness

Formula: Hook + emotion + relatable situation + soft CTA.

Example direction:

- "Có những món quà không cần lớn, chỉ cần đúng người nhận."
- CTA: "Xem gợi ý làm quà".

Use on:

- Homepage hero.
- TikTok/Reels.
- Facebook storytelling posts.

### Consideration

Formula: Problem + options + recommendation + proof.

Example direction:

- "Nếu bạn chưa biết chọn mẫu nào, hãy bắt đầu từ người nhận: thích dễ thương, thích nhẹ nhàng, hay cần món treo hằng ngày."
- CTA: "Nhắn shop gu người nhận".

Use on:

- `/moc-khoa-len`.
- Product collection.
- Blog guides.

### Conversion

Formula: Product fit + price clarity + order steps + reassurance + CTA.

Example direction:

- "Mẫu này hợp làm quà sinh nhật nhỏ xinh. Bạn có thể đổi màu trước khi shop bắt đầu làm."
- CTA: "Nhắn Zalo đặt mẫu này".

Use on:

- Product pages.
- Final CTA.
- Contact/order section.

### Retention

Formula: Thank you + care tip + invitation + next occasion.

Use on:

- After-order messages.
- Social stories.
- Email/message flow.

## Section-Level Content Formulas

| Section | Formula | Reason | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hero | AIDA | Capture attention, create desire, then route to product/order. | `Xem mẫu`, `Nhắn shop tư vấn`. |
| Choose by need | Jobs-to-be-done | Buyers think by recipient/occasion, not SKU. | `Xem mẫu làm quà`. |
| Featured products | FAB | Feature, advantage, benefit makes product cards clearer. | `Xem chi tiết`. |
| Handmade process | Storytelling + EEAT | Shows effort and human craft. | `Xem cách đặt màu`. |
| Personalization | BAB | Before: generic gift; After: personal color; Bridge: message shop. | `Gửi màu bạn thích`. |
| Trust signals | Risk reversal | Reduces uncertainty. | `Xem FAQ`. |
| Review/photo proof | Social proof | Only if real. | `Xem ảnh khách gửi`. |

| Final CTA | Direct response | One clear next action. | `Nhắn Zalo đặt hàng`. |

## Copy System

### Voice

- Warm.
- Clear.
- Lightly playful.
- Gift-first.
- Honest about handmade limits.

### Words to use

- "món quà nhỏ"
- "được chọn màu riêng"
- "shop xác nhận trước khi làm"
- "đóng gói gọn để tặng"
- "nhắn shop gu người nhận"
- "mẫu nhỏ xinh"
- "làm thủ công từng mũi"

### Words to avoid

- "rẻ nhất"
- "xịn nhất"
- "hot trend số 1"
- "cháy hàng" if not real.
- "đảm bảo giống 100%" for handmade.

## CTA Formula

CTA = action + immediate benefit + channel/context.

Examples:

- `Nhắn Zalo để shop giúp chọn màu`
- `Xem mẫu làm quà sinh nhật`
- `Gửi mẫu này cho shop`
- `Xem sản phẩm từ 45k`
- `Hỏi shop thời gian làm`

Avoid:

- Generic "Click here".
- Too many equal-weight CTAs.
- "Mua ngay" if there is no checkout.

## FAQ Formula

Every FAQ answer should follow:

1. Direct answer.
2. Constraint or condition.
3. Next action.

Example:

Question: "Có đổi màu được không?"  
Answer formula: "Có. Bạn gửi tone màu hoặc ảnh tham khảo; shop sẽ xác nhận lại trước khi làm. Nếu mẫu có chi tiết quá nhỏ, shop sẽ báo trước phần có thể điều chỉnh."

## Review Formula

Only use verified reviews.

Format:

- Real customer permission.
- Product ordered.
- Context: gift/self-use/group order.
- Short quote.
- Optional photo if allowed.

If no reviews:

- Use process proof instead: packaging photos, behind-the-scenes, order steps.

## Blog Formula

1. Opening situation.
2. Buyer problem.
3. Criteria.
4. Examples.
5. Product/category links.
6. FAQ.
7. Soft CTA.

## Social Content Formula

### Facebook

Hook -> story -> product fit -> order guidance -> inbox CTA.

### TikTok/Reels

Visual hook -> process -> reveal -> emotional line -> comment/inbox CTA.

### Instagram carousel

Cover hook -> 3-5 tips -> product examples -> CTA/save.
