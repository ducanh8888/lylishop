# LyliShop Content System

Ngay tao: 2026-07-01

Pham vi: he thong noi dung cho website, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, blog, email, landing page va cham soc khach hang. Tai lieu nay khong sao chep cau chu, hinh anh, mau sac, bo cuc hay thuong hieu cua bat ky website tham khao nao.

## 1. Tom Tat Huong Di

LyliShop khong nen ban "moc khoa len" nhu mot mon phu kien don le. LyliShop nen ban cam giac "minh da chon mot mon qua nho nhung co tam".

Thong diep trung tam:

> LyliShop lam nhung mon qua len nho xinh, duoc chon mau va hoan thien rieng de nguoi nhan cam thay minh duoc nghi toi.

Vai tro noi dung:

- Website: giai thich ro san pham, quy trinh dat hang, gia tri qua tang va ly do tin.
- Facebook: ke chuyen, tu van, tao hoi thoai va chot don qua inbox.
- TikTok: tao cam xuc nhanh bang qua trinh lam, dong goi, truoc/sau, tinh huong tang qua.
- Instagram: xay aesthetic, moodboard qua tang, bo suu tap mau va khoanh khac thanh pham.
- Blog/SEO: gom nhu cau tim kiem theo "qua tang", "handmade", "moc khoa len", "sinh nhat", "nguoi yeu", "ban than".
- Email/message: cham soc sau khi khach hoi, sau khi chot don, sau khi nhan hang.

## 2. Bai Hoc Tu Website Tham Khao

### BongBanHaNoi

Diem manh nen hoc:

- Cau truc danh muc rat ro, chia theo nhu cau va loai san pham.
- CTA truc dien: shop now, them gio hang, hotline, Zalo/Facebook.
- Niem tin duoc xay bang hotline, COD, bao hanh, dia chi, chinh sach.
- Co lop noi dung giao duc: tin tuc, huong dan chon san pham, kien thuc cho nguoi mua.

Ap dung cho LyliShop:

- Khong can them gio hang neu LyliShop van ban qua tin nhan.
- Nen them "chon theo nhu cau": lam qua, treo balo, dat nhom, tu thuong.
- Nen co noi dung "huong dan chon mau", "chon qua theo nguoi nhan", "cach dat hang".
- Trust signals can cu the hon: xac nhan mau truoc khi lam, dong goi qua, thoi gian hoan thien, giao hang toan quoc, kenh lien he that.

### Lunessa

Diem manh nen hoc:

- Dinh vi ban phong cach va ban sac ca nhan, khong chi ban san pham.
- Ngon ngu huong den cam giac cua phai dep: toi gian, thanh lich, hien dai, tu tin.
- Co lop "ve chung toi", FAQ, thuong hieu va gu tham my.

Ap dung cho LyliShop:

- Dinh vi LyliShop la "mon qua nho co cam xuc rieng", khong phai shop phu kien gia re.
- Moi bo suu tap nen co mood: ngot ngao, nhe nhang, tinh nghich, am ap, toi gian.
- Noi dung nen giup khach thay "mau nay giong nguoi nhan nao", "dip nao hop", "cam xuc nao hop".

### Nen Thom Teapea

Diem manh nen hoc:

- Ban trai nghiem va cam giac qua mui huong.
- Co cau truc shop theo dong san pham, set qua tang, lien he, tin tuc, theo doi don, wishlist.
- Noi dung nhan vao "doc ban" va "qua tang", phu hop san pham cam xuc.

Ap dung cho LyliShop:

- Chuyen tu "mau moc khoa" sang "set qua tang nho": set ban than, set nguoi yeu, set sinh nhat, set tu thuong.
- Wishlist co the thay bang "luu mau yeu thich" sau nay, nhung hien tai co the lam bang CTA "gui anh mau nay cho shop".
- Noi dung nen mo ta cam giac: mem tay, nho xinh, duoc lam rieng, goi gon gang.

## 3. Bo Nhan Dien Giong Van

Tinh cach giong van:

- Am ap, gan gui, nu tinh vua du, khong sen qua.
- Nho nhe, cham chut, co tam.
- Tu van nhu mot nguoi ban co gu, khong ep mua.
- Noi ro thong tin khi can: gia, size, thoi gian lam, cach dat.

Nen dung:

- "nho xinh", "duoc lam rieng", "cham chut", "hop de tang", "chon mau theo gu", "goi gon gang", "nhan shop tu van".
- Cau ngan, de doc tren mobile.
- Noi bang loi ich cam xuc truoc, thong tin san pham sau.

Khong nen dung:

- "dep nhat thi truong", "cao cap so 1", "mua ngay keo het" lap lai qua day.
- Claim khong co bang chung: review ao, rating ao, so luong ban ao.
- Ngon ngu qua tre con neu dang noi voi nguoi mua qua tang.

Cong thuc cau noi thuong hieu:

> Mot mon qua nho, nhung duoc chon mau va lam rieng de nguoi nhan thay minh duoc quan tam.

## 4. Content Pillars

1. Qua tang co cam xuc
   Noi dung: qua sinh nhat, qua ban than, qua nguoi yeu, qua cam on, qua tu thuong.

2. Handmade va qua trinh lam
   Noi dung: tung mui moc, chon len, lap form, hoan thien chi tiet, dong goi.

3. Chon mau va ca nhan hoa
   Noi dung: mau theo tinh cach, mau theo cung hoang dao, mau theo style, mau theo balo/tui.

4. San pham va bo suu tap
   Noi dung: thỏ, dau tay, tulip, ban chan meo, set dong gia, set nhom.

5. Giao duc khach hang
   Noi dung: moc khoa len la gi, crochet la gi, cach bao quan, size, gia, thoi gian lam.

6. Niem tin va quy trinh
   Noi dung: shop xac nhan truoc khi lam, cach dat hang, dong goi, giao hang, FAQ.

7. Social proof that
   Noi dung: anh don that, feedback that neu co, cau chuyen don hang, hau truong goi don. Khong tao review ao.

## 5. Cong Thuc Hook

### Hook cam xuc

- "Co nhung mon qua khong can lon, chi can dung nguoi nhan."
- "Neu ban chua biet tang gi, hay bat dau tu mau nguoi ay thich."
- "Mot chiec moc khoa nho co the di theo nguoi ay moi ngay."

### Hook tinh huong

- "Sap sinh nhat ban than ma ngan sach khong qua lon?"
- "Muon tang nguoi yeu mot mon qua nho nhung co dau an rieng?"
- "Can qua cho ca nhom ban ma van de thuong va de chia ngan sach?"

### Hook qua trinh

- "Tu mot cuon len thanh mon qua nho trong vai gio."
- "Don nay khach chon tone hong vi nguoi nhan thich do cute."
- "Dong goi mot mon qua nho truoc khi gui di."

### Hook SEO/social

- "Moc khoa len handmade hop lam qua cho ai?"
- "Chon moc khoa len theo mau nhu the nao?"
- "Qua handmade nho xinh duoi 100k nen chon gi?"

## 6. Cong Thuc Storytelling

Cong thuc 1: Nguoi nhan - Ky niem - Mon qua

1. Ai la nguoi nhan?
2. Hai nguoi co ky niem/gu/mau sac gi?
3. LyliShop bien dieu do thanh mau len nho.
4. Ket: mon qua nho nhung co ly do de ton tai.

Mau:

> Ban ay thich mau hong nhe, hay deo balo di hoc va me nhung mon nho xinh. Vay nen thay vi mot mon qua lon, minh chon mot moc khoa len co tone mau rieng. Nho thoi, nhung ngay nao cung co the mang theo.

Cong thuc 2: Truoc - Trong luc - Sau

1. Truoc: khach chua biet chon qua.
2. Trong luc: shop tu van mau, size, chi tiet.
3. Sau: thanh pham duoc dong goi va gui di.

Cong thuc 3: Vat lieu - Ban tay - Cam xuc

1. Soi len/mau len.
2. Qua trinh moc/khau/dong goi.
3. Cam giac khi nhan qua.

## 7. Cong Thuc CTA

CTA nen co 4 nhom:

1. CTA kham pha:
   - Xem mau phu hop
   - Xem bo suu tap
   - Chon theo nhu cau

2. CTA tu van:
   - Nhan shop tu van mau
   - Gui mau ban thich
   - Hoi shop mau hop voi nguoi nhan

3. CTA chot don:
   - Nhan Zalo de dat mau
   - Inbox Facebook de chot mau
   - Gui thong tin dat hang

4. CTA giam do du:
   - Xem cach dat hang
   - Xem FAQ truoc khi dat
   - Xem goi y lam qua

Cong thuc CTA:

> Dong tu hanh dong + loi ich ngay + ngu canh dat hang.

Vi du:

- "Nhan Zalo de shop giup chon mau"
- "Gui anh mau nay de shop bao thoi gian lam"
- "Xem set qua tang cho ban than"

## 8. Cong Thuc Bai Facebook

### Dang bai ke chuyen don hang

1. Hook tinh huong.
2. Nguoi nhan/dip tang.
3. Ly do chon mau/mau san pham.
4. Thanh pham/dong goi.
5. CTA inbox.

Mau:

> Sap sinh nhat ban than nhung khong muon tang mot mon qua qua dai tra?
>
> Mau nay hop voi nhung ban thich do nho xinh, me tone mau nhe va hay deo balo/tui tote. Shop co the doi mau than, mau no hoac them chi tiet nho de mon qua giong nguoi nhan hon.
>
> Muon shop goi y mau theo tinh cach nguoi nhan, inbox LyliShop nhe.

### Dang bai tu van

1. Van de: chua biet chon qua.
2. 3 lua chon theo nguoi nhan.
3. Loi khuyen ngan.
4. CTA gui thong tin nguoi nhan.

### Dang bai uu dai

1. Uu dai ro.
2. Dieu kien ro.
3. Ly do nen dat luc nay.
4. CTA nhanh.

## 9. Cong Thuc Bai TikTok

### Format 1: Lam tu dau den cuoi

- 0-2s: hook text tren man hinh.
- 3-8s: chon len/mau.
- 9-18s: moc va hoan thien chi tiet.
- 19-25s: dong goi.
- 26-30s: CTA "comment mau nguoi ay thich".

Hook:

- "Lam mot mon qua nho cho co gai thich mau hong."
- "Don nay la qua sinh nhat cho ban than."
- "Tu soi len thanh chiec moc khoa di hoc moi ngay."

### Format 2: POV

- "POV: Ban muon tang qua nhung khong muon qua dai tra."
- "POV: Nguoi ay thich do cute nhung ban khong biet mua gi."

### Format 3: Chon mau

- Dua 3 combo mau.
- Hoi nguoi xem chon combo nao.
- CTA comment/inbox.

## 11. Cong Thuc Blog

Moi blog nen theo cau truc:

1. Mo dau bang tinh huong that.
2. Giai thich van de nguoi mua dang gap.
3. Dua tieu chi chon.
4. Goi y mau/san pham lien quan.
5. FAQ ngan.
6. CTA mem ve Zalo/Facebook hoac trang san pham.

Chu de uu tien:

- Moc khoa len handmade la gi?
- Qua handmade tang ban gai nen chon gi?
- Qua sinh nhat nho xinh duoi 100k.
- Cach chon mau qua theo tinh cach.
- Moc khoa len co ben khong?
- Qua tang ban than nho nhung co y nghia.

## 12. Cong Thuc SEO

Moi trang SEO can co:

- 1 intent chinh.
- H1 ro intent.
- Mo dau tra loi nhanh.
- Cac section theo cau hoi nguoi mua.
- Internal link ve san pham, huong dan, lien he.
- FAQ visible va schema neu noi dung hien tren trang.
- CTA dat hang bang tin nhan.

Nhom keyword:

- Commercial: moc khoa len handmade, moc khoa len gia tot, moc khoa len dong gia, dat moc khoa len.
- Gift intent: qua tang handmade, qua sinh nhat handmade, qua tang ban gai, qua tang ban than.
- Informational: moc khoa len la gi, crochet la gi, cach bao quan do len.
- Personalization: moc khoa len theo yeu cau, chon mau moc khoa len, qua handmade theo mau.

## 14. Cong Thuc Email Marketing

LyliShop co the dung email hoac message flow thay cho email neu chua co he thong email.

Flow 1: Khach moi hoi mau

1. Cam on va hoi nhu cau.
2. Gui 3 goi y mau.
3. Hoi thoi gian can nhan.
4. Chot size/gia/thong tin nhan hang.

Flow 2: Sau khi chot don

- Xac nhan mau, mau sac, so luong, gia, thoi gian lam.
- Nhac: handmade co the co chenhlech nho.

Flow 3: Sau khi giao

- Hoi khach da nhan hang chua.
- Goi y cach bao quan.
- Moi gui anh/feedback neu khach thoai mai.

Flow 4: Tai mua/dip le

- Goi y qua theo dip.
- Nhac khach co the dat mau rieng.

## 18. He Thong FAQ

FAQ nen chia thanh nhom:

San pham:

- Moc khoa len co phai handmade 100% khong?
- San pham co chenhlech kich thuoc/mau sac khong?
- Chat lieu len la gi?
- Moc khoa co ben khi treo balo khong?

Tuy chinh:

- Co doi mau duoc khong?
- Co them chu cai/no/tim/hoa duoc khong?
- Co dat theo anh tham khao duoc khong?

Dat hang:

- Dat hang nhu the nao?
- Can gui shop thong tin gi?
- Bao lau shop lam xong?
- Co dat so luong duoc khong?

Giao hang:

- Shop co giao toan quoc khong?
- Phi ship tinh nhu the nao?
- Can dat truoc bao lau neu lam qua sinh nhat?

Qua tang:

- Co dong goi qua khong?
- Co viet loi nhan khong?
- Nen chon mau nao neu chua biet gu nguoi nhan?

## 19. He Thong Xay Dung Niem Tin

Trust signals LyliShop nen uu tien:

- Handmade that: anh/video qua trinh lam.
- Quy trinh ro: chon mau -> xac nhan -> lam -> dong goi -> giao.
- Gia ro: gia tu 45k, ghi chu thay doi theo size/do chi tiet.
- Tuy chinh co gioi han: noi ro cai gi lam duoc, cai gi can hoi truoc.
- Dong goi qua: anh that cua goi hang.
- Kenh lien he that: Zalo, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok.
- Feedback that neu co: chi dang khi duoc khach cho phep.
- Chinh sach ky vong: handmade co net rieng, khong giong 100% may moc.

## 20. He Thong Cham Soc Khach Hang

Truoc khi mua:

- Hoi nguoi nhan la ai.
- Hoi dip tang.
- Hoi mau/thoi gian can nhan.
- Goi y 2-3 mau, khong dua qua nhieu lua chon.

Trong khi lam:

- Xac nhan mau va gia.
- Neu don tuy chinh, gui cap nhat khi can.

Sau khi gui:

- Gui ma van don neu co.
- Nhac cach bao quan.

Sau khi nhan:

- Hoi hang da den on chua.
- Moi khach gui anh/feedback neu thay vui.
- Goi y dat set doi/set nhom cho dip sau.

## 22. Tai Su Dung Mot Noi Dung Thanh Nhieu Dinh Dang

Noi dung goc: "Mot don hang qua sinh nhat cho ban than tone hong."

Tai su dung:

- TikTok: video 30s qua trinh lam va dong goi.
- Instagram Reel: ban cat nhanh co nhac nhe.
- Instagram Story: poll "ban thich hong phan hay hong dau?"
- Facebook post: cau chuyen vi sao khach chon mau nay.
- Carousel: 3 ly do mau hong hop lam qua sinh nhat.
- Blog section: vi du trong bai "chon qua theo tinh cach".
- Product page: them copy "phu hop lam qua sinh nhat".
- Email/message: mau tin nhan goi y dat hang.

Quy trinh:

1. Quay/chup qua trinh lam.
2. Ghi lai: nguoi nhan, dip tang, mau sac, ly do chon.
3. Tach thanh 1 video ngan, 1 post ke chuyen, 1 carousel tu van, 3 story.
4. Gan CTA phu hop tung kenh.
